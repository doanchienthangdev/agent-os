# 04-content — Content Strategy & Production Pillar

> Ideation, drafting, editorial, multi-surface publishing.

**Status:** scaffolded / not yet operating in this org

## What this pillar does (when populated)

- Editorial calendar and topic clusters
- Long-form content drafting (blog, whitepaper, eBook)
- Short-form content (social, newsletter snippets)
- Multi-surface adaptation (one source piece → blog + thread + email + video script)
- Voice consistency across pieces
- Content performance analysis loop

## Roles primarily working in this pillar

- `content-orchestrator` — owns the pillar
- `content-drafter` — produces text drafts (cross-cutting, called by content + growth + customer)
- `editorial-reviewer` — voice + brand check before publishing
- `content-publisher` — the role that ships (separate from drafter; HITL gating)
