## How to populate this file

The boilerplate ships this as a stub. To make it your brand voice:

1. Pick a dominant voice characteristic (the single rule).
2. Rate the 5 tone dimensions — be honest, not aspirational.
3. List 3+ DO / 3+ DON'T moves with concrete examples from your category.
4. Add 4-5 banned words you actively reject.
5. Write 3-4 customer-facing samples; future content agents grade themselves against these.

Once filled in, every content drafter reads this before producing customer-facing copy.
